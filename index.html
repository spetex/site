Frontend Development

I am currently employed as a frontend developer at a Czech event/culture guide and ticket selling company GoOut. It is an awesome product and you should probably check it out if you live in central Europe!

I work with JavaScript or TypeScript in my day to day work. Mostly building web apps using mainstream frameworks like React, Angular or Vue.js. I do not prefer any particular framework or library. My preference is clean, well tested code and good architecture.

Outside of work I have coded projects in Python, Rust, PHP and most recently Ruby. I like to educate myself in computer science topics and learn new computer languages. It gives me a fresh perspective on my day job.

I am a proud Linux operating system user. My distribution of choice is Gentoo which I use on my personal computer, my home Raspberry Pi and a server hosted on Iceland.

Miniature Painting

Miniature painting is my free time go to activity after work and on weekends. Passion for this hobby is slowly replacing my passion for programming. Instead of staying late to code I now stay late to paint.

I am aspiring to replace my day to day software development job with activites related to miniature painting. My plan is very simple:

  1. Creating and participating instead of consuming.
    • Paint every day!
    • Instagram channel documenting my progress
    • Blog with tutorials and my thoughts on hobby
    • Active participation in the community including contests
  2. Establishing a viable business.
    • Commission work
    • Youtube Channel with tutorials
    • Patreon

At the time of writing this I am starting first phase of the plan and it should be my main focus for the upcoming year 2020. Even this site is part of the plan, as I wanted to have a public pledge and record.
