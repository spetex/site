Frontend Development

I am currently employed as a frontend developer at a Czech event/culture guide and ticket selling company GoOut. It is an awesome product and you should probably check it out if you live in central Europe!

I do enjoy programming. I work with JavaScript or TypeScript in my day to day work. Mostly in context with mainstream frameworks like React, Angular or Vue.js. I do not prefer any particular framework or library. My preference is clean, well tested code and good architecture.

Outside of work I have coded projects in Python, Rust, PHP and most recently Ruby. I like to educate myself in topics from computer science and learn new computer languages. It gives me a fresh perspective on my day job.

I do love Linux operating system. My distribution of choice is Gentoo. I use it on my personal computer, my home Raspberry Pi and a server hosted on Iceland. Oftentimes I do enjoy tinkering with linux components like kernel or different filesystems.

Miniature Painting

Miniature painting is my go to activity in my free time after work and on weekends. I got hooked in with the hobby in February 2018 and I am there it ever since.

For me as a nature of my occupation is to sit and stare into a computer screen for more than 8 hours every day. This fact is taking a toll on my posture and sight. After couple of years in this trade I got really tired of it. I have started to not carry my smartphone or practicing analog evenings from time to time. In one of those evenigs I came to a realization. I need a unplugged - no internet, no computer hobby. Preferably using my hands. I tried many things: playing the drums being one of those but not a single one quite clicked with me so I left it at that.

In February 2018 I was walking the streets of Edinburgh and spotted an interesting shop. It was a Games Workshop Hobby Store. I went in and I instantly knew this is it. This is the thing I was looking for a long time. Right after I came back from my vacation in Scotland I bought my first Warhammer 40k rulebook, first model and some paints. I was extatic.

Fast forward to 2020 my passion for miniature painting is slowly outgrowing my passion for computers. As I am getting older I am looking for some thing I can do to earn my money on my own. Programming and software development side projects are lacking the material aspect for me. I love to work with my hands.

I have decided that I will make this hobby also my living.
